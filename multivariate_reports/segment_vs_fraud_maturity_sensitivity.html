Key Observations

  • Observation Changing the maturity cutoff affects the level of the observed fraud rates, but it does not overturn the relative ranking of the segments, which suggests the segment risk ranking is not only a recency artifact.
  • Observation The higher relative fraud rate for less mature Segment 4 transactions suggests that exclusion decisions for new BIN-Channel pairs in this segment should be more tolerant or require more maturity before acting.
  • Observation One possible explanation is that Segment 4 is more aggressive and its fraud tends to be detected faster, which would make it look disproportionately worse on more recent transaction cohorts.
